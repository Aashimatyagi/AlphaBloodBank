Alpha BloodBank

Alpha BloodBank enforces Drug & Cosmetic Act, National blood policy standards and guidelines ensuring proper collection & donation, effective management and monitoring the quality and quantity of the donated blood. Considering the national roll out, e-Rakt Kosh has been developed with modular and scalable approach with configurable rule based architecture allowing customization to easily incorporate specific requirements from nationwide stakeholders.
This website aims at:

  • The Donor Management System for identifying, tracking and blocking donors based on donor's health, donation history etc.

  • A centralized Blood Inventory Management System for keeping track of the blood stock across numerous blood banks.

  • Generation of rare blood group donor registries and the generation of regular repeat donors
